Photography Tips

  • Use Natural Light: Golden hours give soft, flattering light.
  • Keep It Simple: Remove distractions from your frame.
  • Experiment With Angles: Change perspective for fresh shots.
  • Edit with Care: Enhance, but stay true to reality.
  • Practice: The best way to improve is to shoot constantly.
